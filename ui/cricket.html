Rules

  • Each team will be of 11 players with atleast 4 women on each side.
  • Each match will be of 6 overs per innings and must complete within 30 minutes (If not completed within time, slow over rates may apply).
  • Only women can opt for help runners.
  • First over should be bowled by a woman and only a woman can bat in the first over.
  • No leg bye runs.
  • No free hit offered for a No ball.
  • If the ball bounces pass the head it will be a No ball.
  • A Bowler can bowl maximum of 1 over.
  • Rest cricketing rules apply.
  • Umpires will be from batting team.
  • Incase of tie there will be a super over where 6 bowlers are allowed to bowl a ball each and number of hits to the stumps determine the winner.
  • 5 mins break between the innings.

Rules

  • There is no special category for the type of dessert you can bring.
  • So bring a dessert that you like, comfortable making, easy to make and you think will win. No need to bring something expensive.
  • Quantity/size - enough to feed about 10 people.
  • Please list the name of the dessert and the ingredients in it. Tent cards for desserts can be collected from Leeza.
  • All dessert entries must be anonymous. So please DO NOT put your name on it.
  • Please bring a serving utensil appropriate for your dessert, i.e. cake/pie a pie server . custard/porridge . a ladle and so on.
  • You must be in it to win it. Win $50 Prize.
